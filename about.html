ABOUT US

Since 2001 we are a successful woodworking company, working whole over Ukraine regions, offering various timber products to meet it's customers' needs. We are producers of European hardwood and softwood species: Oak, Birch, Pine, and Linden.

Our focus is the perfect quality of ready goods, strict quality control, well-developed and optimized logistics systems, that enable us to deliver our products in different parts of the world by land or sea.

Satisfaction of our customers is our priority. As a customer, you are at the center of our attention. We value long-term partnership and we do our best to deliver high quality products on the shortest time possible with maximum flexibility for our clients.

On our daily work we value good working climate and sustainable actions. We believe that a good working atmosphere encourages everyone to pursue the highest levels of motivation and productivity. We are convinced that this approach will enable us successfully manage our company.

Everyday we are working hard to maintain our values and to reach sustainable goals.
